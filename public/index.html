Server Rules


  1. Block Placement Restrictions:

    • Do not place blocks anywhere in the world. If staff or admins find unauthorized block placements, there is a high chance you will receive a temporary ban.


  2. Netherite Grinding Prohibition:

    • Grinding for netherite is prohibited on this server. Possession of netherite or ancient debris will result in a serious ban.


  3. PvP Guidelines:

    • PvP is allowed, but you are not permitted to steal items after killing someone. Killing other players will place a bounty on your head, encouraging other SMP players to hunt you and claim rewards.


  4. Netherite Grinding Prohibition:

    • Griefing or destroying other players' houses will result in a permanent ban.


  5. Argument Resolution:

    • Keep any arguments or fights within the SMP. Reach out to admins for help rather than fighting on Discord. Engaging in fights on Discord will lead to a permanent ban.


  6. Respect and Abuse:

    • Be respectful to all players on the SMP. Abuse in the SMP chat will not be tolerated.

  7. Base Placement:
    • Be respectful to all players on the SMP. Abuse in the SMP chat will not be tolerated.


By following these rules, you help ensure a positive and enjoyable experience for everyone on the server. Thank you for your cooperation!
